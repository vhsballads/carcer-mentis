Hello,

I'm Sena. I'm 19 years old. I am a student at MacEwan University. I am an Oriental Orthodox Christian. My hobbies include cycling, chess, photography, and anything to do with computers.

I’ve been trying to make a more regular habit out of reading and writing again following the pandemic, so I began writing blog posts around 4 years ago in order to cement that habit into my daily life, or at least attempt to do so. I recall setting up my first blogger page when I was around 15 years old, and forgetting about it til the resolution to start reading and writing came to mind afterwards; my blog on there initially consisted of book and movie reviews. It wasn't anything notable, and later on I began looking into other ~~more intuitive~~ platforms for blogging. I’ve reconstructed and changed platforms too many times to count, and I archived most previous articles with the intent of polishing or reworking their content someday. I’ll work on more posts over the next year and might someday commit to periodic posts, but for now, think of this website as a somewhat organised extension of my notes, essays, and musings.
